Our Pizza Making Process and Ingredients

The Art of Pizza Making at Fetta di Beatitudine

At Fetta di Beatitudine, we believe that every pizza should be a masterpiece, a true slice of bliss. Our process combines traditional Italian techniques with a passion for quality, ensuring that every bite you take is a journey to culinary heaven.

Our Pizza Making Process

Here's a glimpse into our pizza-making process and the premium ingredients we use

Handcrafted Dough: The foundation of a great pizza is its dough. We start with the finest Italian flour, which is carefully selected for its perfect balance of strength and elasticity. Our dough is handmade daily, using a time-honored recipe that includes a slow fermentation process. This not only enhances the flavor but also ensures a light and airy crust with a perfect chew.

Homemade Sauce: Our pizza sauce is made from scratch using ripe, juicy tomatoes, harvested at their peak. We blend them with a mix of fresh herbs and spices to create a rich and flavorful base. The secret to our sauce lies in its simplicity—no artificial additives, just pure, natural goodness.

Fresh, Premium Ingredients: We believe that the best pizzas start with the best ingredients. That's why we source our toppings from local farms and trusted suppliers. From farm-fresh vegetables to artisanal cheeses and top-quality meats, every ingredient is selected for its superior taste and texture.

Perfect Assembly: Each Pizza is meticulously assembled to ensure a harmonious balance of flavors in every bite. We start with a generous layer of our homemade sauce, followed by a sprinkling of premium cheese, and finish with your choice of fresh toppings. Our skilled pizzaiolos (pizza makers) take great care to distribute the toppings evenly, so every slice is a delightful experience.

Stone Oven Baking: The magic happens in our stone ovens, where each pizza is baked to perfection. The high temperatures of the stone oven ensure a crispy, golden crust while keeping the toppings juicy and flavorful. This traditional method of baking brings out the best in each ingredient, creating a pizza that's both crispy and tender.

Our Ingredients

Italian Flour: Our dough is made using the finest Italian flour, known for its superior quality and perfect texture. This flour is the key to our dough’s light and airy structure, providing a delicious base for our pizzas.

Fresh Tomatoes: Only the ripest, juiciest tomatoes make it into our sauce. These tomatoes are packed with flavor and provide a natural sweetness that perfectly complements the other ingredients.

Artisanal Cheese: We use a variety of artisanal cheeses, including creamy mozzarella, tangy gorgonzola, and rich parmesan. Each cheese is chosen for its exceptional taste and melting qualities, ensuring every pizza is cheesy and delicious.

Local and Organic Produce: Our vegetables are sourced from local farms that practice organic farming methods. From crisp bell peppers to earthy mushrooms and spicy arugula, our produce is always fresh and bursting with flavor.

Premium Meats: For our meat lovers, we offer a selection of premium meats, including savory pepperoni, spicy Italian sausage, and tender prosciutto. Each meat is carefully selected for its quality and taste, adding a rich and satisfying element to our pizzas.

Fresh Herbs and Spices: We use a blend of fresh herbs and spices to enhance the flavor of our pizzas. Basil, oregano, garlic, and rosemary are just a few of the ingredients that add depth and complexity to our dishes.
