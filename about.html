About us

Our story

At Fetta di Beatitudine, our journey began with a passion for creating the perfect pizza.

Founded by Tiburogio Kopssi in 2010, our pizzeria was built on the belief that every pizza should be a masterpiece, a true slice of bliss.

From humble beginnings, we have grown into a beloved pizzeria known for our commitment to quality, tradition, and exceptional taste.

  • Homemade Sauce
  • Fresh, Premium Ingredients
  • Perfect Assembly
  • Stone Oven Baking
